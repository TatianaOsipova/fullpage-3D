About Us

Atex Group has a unique experience in repairing various types of 0.1 kW to 250 kW asynchronous motors, domestic and foreign electric motors, DC motors, crane and elevator electric motors, explosion-proof electric motors, asphalt spreader generators, magnetic coils, refrigeration compressors, and high-pressure vessels, as well as in repairing pumps, electric motors without winding and datasheet specifications requiring complex repairs and a custom-tailored approach.

Pump Maintenance

We help you with all pump maintenance services GRUNDFOS, KSB, WILO, and PEDROLLO (replacement of mechanical seals, sealing rings, gaskets).

Repair Electric Motors

We carry out repair of electric motors of elevators and cranes of any type, modernization of domestic and foreign electrical machines, rewinding of electric motors.

Repair of DC Motors

Atex Group provides thorough in-house AC and DC motor repairs and rebuilds. Trained, experienced technicians oversee and perform the work, from bearing changes and rewinding to dynamic balancing and dyno testing.
